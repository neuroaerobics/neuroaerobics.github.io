Neuroaerobics opens up new brain cells that have previously not been used before. Neuroaerobics is doing mundane actions in a new way, such as brushing your teeth with the hand you don't usually use, learning how to type, smelling things that you never have smelled before. These mundane tasks will open up brain cells that were previously not used and maybe even some new pathways as well. Whether this increases intelligence remains to be seen, but if you are losing brain cells through natural aging your only defense is to open new ones.
